Problem Statement 1
According to a report by the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, as of the second quarter of 2021, the total outstanding student loan debt in the United States was $1.57 trillion. The same report found that the delinquency rate for student loan payments was 6.5%, meaning that 6.5% of borrowers were at least 90 days behind on their payments. 
Colaberry School of Data Analytics provides a subscription-based service to its students, where they pay a recurring fee to access the service. Without an efficient payment tracking system, the company may experience revenue losses due to missed payments, inaccuracies in financial records, and poor customer service. Therefore, the development of a reliable payment tracking system is critical to the success of the company's subscription-based service.
